Themed dinners

We hosted three awesome dinners during our pre-campaign to welcome the former teams. We were inspired by cultural references we all share, particularly pieces of art that marked our childhood such as Alice in Wonderland 🐱🎂 and Ghibli Studio animations 🍙🎉. We created immersive experience for our guests using food, decorations and games as a way to create an original and enjoyable atmosphere.

The project M

It is completely normal to be both astounded and terrified by our project M : we built a 4-meter-high marionette of Medusa made out of papier-mâché, paint and wooden structure ! It took us several hours of hard work to achieve such a massive piece of art 🐍🚀.
